Recirculation Fan (D24)

Overview

D24 recirculation fan is equipped with efficient motor and aerodynamic housing to maximize the volumetric flow of the fan, more energy efficient compared to conventional circulation fan in the market. D24 adopts volumetric circulation to achieve higher air stirring rate per hour. The housing of fan is highly corrosive resistant with special coating to last for long lifetime. Due to highly efficient flow design of motor and impeller system, the fan is maintenance free. D24 covers not just 2-D area but also 3-D volume of room to create strong vortices!
