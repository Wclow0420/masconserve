Overview

We specialize in offering comprehensive OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and ODM (Original Design Manufacturer) services. As an OEM, we excel in manufacturing high-quality components or products based on your exact specifications, allowing you to retail them under your brand name. As an ODM, we take pride in managing the entire product development lifecycle, from design to manufacturing, ensuring that the final product aligns seamlessly with your vision. Our commitment is to provide tailored manufacturing solutions that empower your brand with quality and innovation.

Various fan housing manufacturing techniques option. Plastic part is produced through Injection moulding mainly, vacuum moulding is optional. Matured partnership with part manufacturer to ensure delivery quality.
Impeller blade is main focus of fan structure where each blade is measured and packed according to weight difference of 1g in set.
FRP part is produced via SMC process mainly, strict selection on raw material quality to ensure good mechanical strength and lifetime.
Other composite materials are strictly selected to ensure good performance on durability and working environment.
Metal part fabrication is completed through precision metal manufacturing equipment.
We have used high quality metal material that can achieve higher lifetime against corrosion.
Each metal part is controlled strictly on the size tolerance and precision.
We have used high corrosion resistance accessories for fastening kits.
