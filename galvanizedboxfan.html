Galvanized Box Fan (S51)

Overview

S51 series fan is an unique high torque performance exhaust fan up to 125 Pa static pressure with steady air flow performance across different pressure. The fan is direct driven with IE 3 IE 4 high efficiency motor. It is perfect for application which needs high air flow and efficiency for wide pressure range. Fan is built for different application such as farming environment and industrial sector. The structure is robust and built for longer life time!
